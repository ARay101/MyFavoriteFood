My Favorite Food: Pizza!

Is your mouth watering for delicious pizza yet? If not, read more!

My favorite food is pizza. It's very delicious. My favorite kind of chicken pizza, with some vegetables and cheese, however, I don't care which sauce it is unless it's spicy.
Pizza is a very popular food, and there are many pizzerias worldwide, including Pizza Hut, Domino's Pizza, Papa John's, Pizza Corner, and Smokin' Joe's.
